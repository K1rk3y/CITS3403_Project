How it works

Step 1: Register and Choose Your Role

Visit our website, and sign up as either a patron or home cook.

Step 2: Request or Post

Patrons: request specific dishes or browse home cooks' postings.

Home Cooks: Create postings for your dishes, detailing ingredients and availability.

Step 3: Order or Respond

Patrons: Place orders from postings or accept offers from cooks.

Home Cooks: Respond to patron requests and manage orders.

Step 4: Enjoy!

Patrons enjoy delicious home-cooked meals, and home cooks share the joy of their cooking.
