How it Works

Step 1: Register and Choose Your Role

Visit our website, and sign up as either a patron or home cook.

Step 2: Request or Post

Patrons: request specific dishes or browse home cooks' postings.

Home Cooks: Create postings for your dishes, detailing ingredients and availability.

Step 3: Order or Respond

Patrons: Place orders from postings or accept offers from cooks.

Home Cooks: Respond to patron requests and manage orders.

Step 4: Enjoy!

Patrons enjoy delicious home-cooked meals, and home cooks share the joy of their cooking.

Empowering local homecooks

Our platform celebrates the talent of local home cooks by providing them a space to share their culinary creations with the community. Home cooks can showcase their dishes, gain recognition, and earn incentives, turning their passion for cooking into a rewarding venture.

Access to healthier, more affordable meals

Patrons can enjoy a variety of homemade meals that are not only more economical than dining out but also healthier and packed with authentic flavors. Our service connects you with local cooks offering delicious, nutritious meals right in your neighborhood.

Supporting community and sustainability

We are committed to fostering a community where food is not just consumed but shared and appreciated. By connecting patrons with nearby home cooks, we help reduce food waste and support local economies, making every meal a step towards a more sustainable world.
